How EmotionSense Works

EmotionSense AI uses deep learning algorithms to analyze facial expressions in images and determine the emotional state being displayed.

Our system is trained on millions of images and can detect three primary emotions:

  • Happy - Smiling, joyful expressions
  • Sad - Frowning, downturned expressions
  • Neutral - Neither happy nor sad expressions

For best results, use clear, well-lit images with the subject facing the camera.
